Habanero Pepper Cream Pasta

Description

This is an entr√©e full of piquant flavor, an spicy cream sauce made with habanero peppers, garlic, and shallots.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cavatappi pasta
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 medium shallot, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, diced
  • 1 dried habanero pepper, chopped
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 large tomato, diced
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil. Add pasta and cook until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes
  2. While the pasta is cooking, melt butter with olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add shallot, garlic, adn habanero pepper; cook and stir until lightly browned and fragrant, about 2 minutes. Remove from heat.
  3. Bring cream to a simmer in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in shallot mixture and tomato, then mix in flour and black pepper. Simmer until thickened, 5 to 8 minutes. Stir in parmesan cheese. Remove from heat and let sit until pasta finished.
  4. Drain pasta. Serve pasta on plates and ladle sauce over the top
